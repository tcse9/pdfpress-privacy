Privacy Policy for PDFPress

Last updated: April 14, 2026

PDFPress ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our iOS app handles your information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

PDFPress does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All PDF processing happens entirely on your device.

  • We do not upload your files to any server.
  • We do not track your usage or behavior.
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics SDKs.

Files & Documents

All PDF files you open, compress, merge, split, protect, unlock, or convert remain on your device at all times. PDFPress processes files locally using Apple's built-in PDFKit framework. Output files are saved to your device's temporary storage and shared via the iOS share sheet — your choice, your control.

Purchases & Subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found at revenuecat.com/privacy.

App Storage

PDFPress uses your device's local storage only to remember preferences (such as whether you have seen the onboarding screen) and to track usage counts for free-tier daily limits. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

PDFPress does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
